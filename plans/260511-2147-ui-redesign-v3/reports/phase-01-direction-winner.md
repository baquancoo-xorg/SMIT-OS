## Aesthetic Philosophy (from Stitch designMd)

> "Executive Precision" + "Spatial Depth". Designed for high-stakes B2B sales operations, rejects flat utilitarian SaaS in favor of "Physical Digital" aesthetic. Inspired by **Apple's spatial computing (visionOS)** and **macOS Sonoma**.
>
> "Bento Grid" philosophy where data is compartmentalized into hyper-organized modular tiles. Emotional response: clarity, premium craftsmanship, effortless control.
>
> Key characteristics:
> - High-transparency glass surfaces
> - Ultra-soft multi-layered shadows
> - Restricted but vibrant accent palette
> - 3D hover transforms (subtle X/Y rotation based on mouse position)
> - Chromatic shadows (low-opacity tinted color, not pure black)
> - Hyper-rounded corners (16-24px)
